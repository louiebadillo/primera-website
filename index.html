FAQs

How quickly should I call after water damage?

Call right away. Mold can start within 24–48 hours (EPA/CDC). Many companies respond 24/7 and can be on-site in 45 minutes to 2 hours—don’t wait for your insurer.

What does water damage restoration typically cost?

Costs vary: minor around $1,200–$3,500, moderate $4,000–$7,500, severe $10,000+, and flooded basements ~$43,000 on average. Many use standard pricing (e.g. Xactimate).

Why does Calgary see so much restoration activity?

Calgary and Alberta have had some of Canada’s costliest weather events. The August 2024 hailstorm caused nearly $3B in insured losses; the June 2020 storm about $1.2B and 63,000 claims (IBC).

Do I need my insurance claim number before work starts?

No. Start mitigation immediately. You’ll need the claim number for billing later; waiting can worsen damage (e.g. mold). Many restorers work directly with insurers.

How long does drying take after water damage?

Often 3–5 days or more for an average room, depending on conditions. Surfaces can feel dry while subfloors stay wet, so professional assessment matters.
